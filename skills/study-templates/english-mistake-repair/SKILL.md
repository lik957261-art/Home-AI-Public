# English Mistake Repair Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Review one prior mistake from cleaned progress signals.
2. Restate why it was wrong.
3. Complete one variant repair.
4. Receive AI confirmation and next-practice guidance.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must name the mistake pattern, explain the correct rule or strategy, require one transferable repair, and avoid repeating the full original private answer.

## Privacy

Store only mistake-pattern summaries, repair summaries, score, and report references. Do not store full child answers, full transcripts, question text, answer keys, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.

# English Short Writing Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Submit a first draft of 6-8 English sentences.
2. Receive AI feedback with concrete rewrite targets.
3. Submit a rewritten version.
4. Add one short reflection sentence.
5. Receive final evaluation, report, and reward settlement.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must include strengths, focus areas, sentence-level issue, why it matters, fix, improved example, rewrite checklist, reflection prompts, and next-practice guidance. The first draft must not complete the card or settle coins.

## Privacy

Store only summaries, score, repair requirements, reward status, and report references. Do not store the full draft or rewrite outside the Kanban comment record.

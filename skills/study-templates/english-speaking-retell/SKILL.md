# English Speaking Retell Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Read or listen to the assigned material.
2. Retell the main idea.
3. Add two supporting details.
4. Retry after AI hints.
5. Review mistake explanation and next-practice guidance.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must cover content order, detail support, fluency, retry repair, and one concrete sentence-level improvement. It must not rely on a score alone.

## Privacy

Store retell summaries and repair summaries only. Do not store full recordings, full transcripts, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.

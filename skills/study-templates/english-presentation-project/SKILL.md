# English Presentation Project Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Prepare an opening, two main points, and a closing.
2. Rehearse or submit the presentation attempt.
3. Repair after AI feedback.
4. Add one reflection sentence.
5. Receive evaluation, report, and reward settlement.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must cover organization, content support, spoken delivery or rehearsal quality, and reflection. It must include one concrete improvement for the next presentation.

## Privacy

Store only outline summaries, rehearsal summaries, repair summaries, score, and report references. Do not store full recordings, full transcripts, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.

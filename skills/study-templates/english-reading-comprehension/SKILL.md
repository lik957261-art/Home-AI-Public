# English Reading Comprehension Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Read the assigned material or summary.
2. Answer the comprehension prompt.
3. Revise after AI feedback.
4. Restate why one mistake happened.
5. Complete one variant repair.

## Feedback Contract

Return strengths, specific gap, why the gap matters, a concrete fix, a better example, a revision checklist, and next-practice guidance. A score alone is not valid feedback.

## Privacy

Store only summaries, event ids, score, repair requirements, and report references. Do not store full answers, full source text, question text, answer keys, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.

# English Shadowing Pronunciation Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Shadow the assigned sentence group.
2. Receive pronunciation, rhythm, or stress gap feedback.
3. Repeat the repaired attempt.
4. Receive evaluation and next-practice guidance.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must identify one or two concrete pronunciation or rhythm targets, provide a repair instruction, and require a repeated attempt.

## Privacy

Store only pronunciation gap summaries, repair summaries, score, and report references. Do not store full recordings, full transcripts, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.

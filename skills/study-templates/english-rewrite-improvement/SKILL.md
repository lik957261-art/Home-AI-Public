# English Rewrite Improvement Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Rewrite the target sentence or short paragraph.
2. Explain what changed and why.
3. Complete one variant repair.
4. Receive final evaluation and next-practice guidance.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must compare the original target summary with the rewrite summary, name the improvement dimension, explain why it is better or still weak, and provide one transferable variant.

## Privacy

Store only rewrite summaries, change-reason summaries, variant summaries, score, and report references. Do not store full child answers, answer keys, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.

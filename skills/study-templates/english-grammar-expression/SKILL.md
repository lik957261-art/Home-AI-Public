# English Grammar Expression Template

Use this template only through Hermes Mobile Growth services.

## Learner Flow

1. Repair the target grammar pattern in short expressions.
2. Write four corrected sentences.
3. Explain the pattern in one simple sentence.
4. Complete one variant repair.
5. Receive evaluation and next-practice guidance.

## Feedback Contract

Feedback must identify the grammar pattern, explain why the original expression fails, provide a corrected example, and test transfer with a variant.

## Privacy

Store only repair summaries, rule summaries, variant summaries, score, and report references. Do not store full answers, full question text, answer keys, raw prompts, local paths, or secrets.
